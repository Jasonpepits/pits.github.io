SERBISYONG MAY PUSO

MONIQUE YAZMIN MARIA “NIK” QUIRINO LAGDAMEO lives a life dedicated to public service. She has distinguished herself as one of the eminent citizens of the Makati City. For over thirty-one years, she has competently and faithfully served the city in various capacities. Nik, as she is fondly called by her constituents, is the youngest daughter of Spouses Militza Quirino-Lagdameo and Enrique Lagdameo. This 47-year-old Presiding Officer of Sangguniang Panlungsod continues to live by and actualized her personal maxim. “Maliit man ako pero pagdating sa paglilingkod malaki ang puso ko.”
